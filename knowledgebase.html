Knowledgebase: Design of social media for knowledge share



Technical University of Crete


In this project I coordinated the design of an online knowledge social media. This application supports sharing, following, combining, gathering of knowledge. It was primarily designed with storyboards which were evaluated with pluralistic walkthroughs.




The paper prototypes of the website were evaluated through pluralistic walkthroughs.
